# Bad News

Decline a request, deliver a delay, or share tough information gracefully.

## Template

```
Write an email delivering difficult news.

Context:
- Recipient: [WHO]
- Our relationship: [CLIENT / PARTNER / VENDOR / etc.]
- The bad news: [WHAT HAPPENED]
- The reason (if shareable): [WHY]
- What I can offer instead: [ALTERNATIVE / NEXT STEPS]

Tone:
- Direct but empathetic
- Take responsibility where appropriate
- No over-apologizing
- Under 100 words
```

## Example

```
Write an email delivering difficult news.

Context:
- Recipient: James, my client
- Our relationship: ongoing project client
- The bad news: The project will be delayed by 2 weeks
- The reason: permit approval took longer than expected
- What I can offer instead: weekly progress updates and priority scheduling once permits clear

Tone:
- Direct but empathetic
- Take responsibility where appropriate
- No over-apologizing
- Under 100 words
```

## When to Use

- Project delays
- Declining a request
- Price increases
- Ending a working relationship
- Any news the recipient won't want to hear
