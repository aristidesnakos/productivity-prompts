# Follow-Up

Nudge someone who hasn't responded without being annoying.

## Template

```
Write a follow-up email.

Context:
- I previously emailed about [TOPIC]
- It's been [TIME SINCE LAST EMAIL]
- My goal is [WHAT YOU WANT]
- Our relationship: [NEW CONTACT / EXISTING CLIENT / etc.]

Tone:
- Friendly, not pushy
- Assume they're busy, not ignoring me
- Under 50 words

Include:
- Brief reminder of what I asked
- Easy way to respond (yes/no question or specific time)
```

## Example

```
Write a follow-up email.

Context:
- I previously emailed about scheduling a site visit
- It's been 5 days since last email
- My goal is to get the visit scheduled this week
- Our relationship: new lead from referral

Tone:
- Friendly, not pushy
- Assume they're busy, not ignoring me
- Under 50 words

Include:
- Brief reminder of what I asked
- Easy way to respond (yes/no question or specific time)
```

## When to Use

- No response after 3-5 days
- Need to move something forward
- Don't want to seem desperate or aggressive
