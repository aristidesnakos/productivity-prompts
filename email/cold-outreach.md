# Cold Outreach

First contact to a potential client or partner.

## Template

```
Write a cold outreach email.

Context:
- I am [YOUR NAME], [YOUR ROLE] at [YOUR COMPANY]
- I help [TARGET AUDIENCE] with [PROBLEM YOU SOLVE]
- The recipient is [THEIR NAME/ROLE] at [THEIR COMPANY]
- I found them via [HOW YOU FOUND THEM]

What I want:
- [SPECIFIC ASK: call, meeting, reply, etc.]

Constraints:
- Under 100 words
- No fluff or flattery
- One clear call to action
- Sound like a human, not a template
```

## Example

```
Write a cold outreach email.

Context:
- I am Sarah Chen, founder at QuoteFlow
- I help contractors send professional quotes in under 5 minutes
- The recipient is Mike Torres, owner at Torres Electrical
- I found them via a LinkedIn post about struggling with paperwork

What I want:
- A 15-minute call to show them the tool

Constraints:
- Under 100 words
- No fluff or flattery
- One clear call to action
- Sound like a human, not a template
```

## When to Use

- Reaching out to someone who doesn't know you
- Partnership inquiries
- Sales outreach
- Networking requests
