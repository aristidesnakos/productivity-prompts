# Quick Reply

Respond to a client email professionally in under 2 minutes.

## Template

```
Reply to this email from my client.

Context:
- I am a [YOUR ROLE] at [YOUR COMPANY]
- My [RELATIONSHIP] with [CLIENT]
- My main objective is to [TALK ABOUT X]
- My tone is professional but warm
- Keep it under 100 words

Their email:
[PASTE EMAIL HERE]

Output:
- Direct response to their question/request
- One clear next step if needed
- Sign off with my name: [YOUR NAME]
```

## Example

```
Reply to this email from my client.

Context:
- I am a project manager at BuildRight Construction
- My relationship with the client is that I manage their kitchen renovation project
- My main objective is to confirm the timeline and address any pending issues
- My tone is professional but warm
- Keep it under 100 words

Their email:
"Hi, just checking in on the timeline for the kitchen renovation. Are we still on track for the March 15th completion date? Also, the tile samples haven't arrived yet."

Output:
Hi [Client's Name],

Yes, we are on track for the March 15th completion date. Regarding the tiles, I've followed up with the supplier, and they should arrive by this Friday. I'll keep you updated.

Please let me know if you have any other concerns.

Best regards,  
Marcus
```

## When to Use

- Client asks a question
- Need to confirm something quickly
- Any email that doesn't require deep thought, just clear communication
