# Handoff

Introduce two people via email.

## Template

```
Write an introduction email connecting two people.

Person A:
- Name: [NAME]
- Role: [WHAT THEY DO]
- Why they're relevant: [WHAT THEY NEED OR OFFER]

Person B:
- Name: [NAME]
- Role: [WHAT THEY DO]
- Why they're relevant: [WHAT THEY NEED OR OFFER]

Why I'm connecting them:
[THE MUTUAL BENEFIT]

Constraints:
- Under 75 words
- Clear reason for the intro
- Explicit next step (who should reach out first)
- BCC myself out after sending
```

## Example

```
Write an introduction email connecting two people.

Person A:
- Name: David
- Role: Owns a landscaping company
- Why they're relevant: Looking for a reliable irrigation specialist

Person B:
- Name: Rachel
- Role: Runs an irrigation installation business
- Why they're relevant: Expanding and looking for landscaping partnerships

Why I'm connecting them:
David needs irrigation for his projects, Rachel needs landscaping partners

Constraints:
- Under 75 words
- Clear reason for the intro
- Explicit next step (who should reach out first)
- BCC myself out after sending
```

## When to Use

- Connecting two people in your network
- Referrals
- Partnership matchmaking
- Helping someone find a service provider
