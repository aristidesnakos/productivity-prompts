# Decision Brief

Get a structured pros/cons analysis for a business decision.

## Template

```
Help me decide: [THE DECISION]

Options I'm considering:
1. [OPTION A]
2. [OPTION B]
3. [OPTION C - if applicable]

Context:
- My situation: [RELEVANT BACKGROUND]
- My priorities: [WHAT MATTERS MOST: cost, speed, quality, risk, etc.]
- My constraints: [BUDGET, TIMELINE, RESOURCES, etc.]

For each option, give me:
- Pros (3-5 bullet points)
- Cons (3-5 bullet points)
- Best for: [what type of person/situation this suits]
- Watch out for: [hidden risks or gotchas]

End with:
- Your recommendation based on my priorities
- One question I should ask myself before deciding
```

## Example

```
Help me decide: Should I hire an employee or use contractors for my growing workload?

Options I'm considering:
1. Hire a full-time employee
2. Use 2-3 regular contractors
3. Hybrid: one part-time employee + contractors for overflow

Context:
- My situation: Solo consultant, revenue growing 30% year over year, turning down projects
- My priorities: Flexibility and quality of work
- My constraints: Can invest $50-70k annually, don't want HR headaches

For each option, give me:
- Pros (3-5 bullet points)
- Cons (3-5 bullet points)
- Best for: [what type of person/situation this suits]
- Watch out for: [hidden risks or gotchas]

End with:
- Your recommendation based on my priorities
- One question I should ask myself before deciding
```

## When to Use

- Hiring decisions
- Tool/software selection
- Business model choices
- Investment decisions
- Any fork-in-the-road moment
