# Competitor Scan

Understand what 3-5 competitors are doing without spending hours on their websites.

## Template

```
Analyze these competitors for me:

Competitors:
1. [COMPANY 1 NAME / URL]
2. [COMPANY 2 NAME / URL]
3. [COMPANY 3 NAME / URL]

For each, tell me:
- What they offer (core products/services)
- Who they target (their ideal customer)
- How they position themselves (their main message/angle)
- Pricing (if publicly available)
- What they do well
- Gaps or weaknesses I could exploit

Context:
- I run [YOUR BUSINESS]
- I'm trying to [YOUR GOAL: differentiate, price competitively, find gaps, etc.]

Output:
- Table format comparing all three
- Summary of the biggest opportunity I should consider
```

## Example

```
Analyze these competitors for me:

Competitors:
1. CleanSweep Pro (cleansweeppro.com)
2. Sparkle Commercial (sparklecommercial.com)
3. OfficeFresh Cleaning (officefresh.com)

For each, tell me:
- What they offer (core products/services)
- Who they target (their ideal customer)
- How they position themselves (their main message/angle)
- Pricing (if publicly available)
- What they do well
- Gaps or weaknesses I could exploit

Context:
- I run a commercial cleaning business in Denver
- I'm trying to find an underserved niche in the market

Output:
- Table format comparing all three
- Summary of the biggest opportunity I should consider
```

## When to Use

- Entering a new market
- Repositioning your business
- Setting or adjusting pricing
- Preparing a competitive pitch
