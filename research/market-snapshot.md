# Market Snapshot

Quickly understand what's happening in an industry right now.

## Template

```
Give me a market snapshot of [INDUSTRY/NICHE].

I need to understand:
1. Current market size and growth trajectory
2. Top 3-5 trends happening right now (2024-2025)
3. Key players (both established and emerging)
4. Biggest challenges or pain points in this market
5. Where the opportunities are

Context:
- I'm a [YOUR ROLE] considering [WHY YOU'RE RESEARCHING]
- My focus is on [SPECIFIC SEGMENT if any]
- Geography: [REGION if relevant]

Output format:
- Bullet points, not paragraphs
- Include sources where possible
- Flag anything that's speculation vs. data-backed
```

## Example

```
Give me a market snapshot of residential solar installation in the US.

I need to understand:
1. Current market size and growth trajectory
2. Top 3-5 trends happening right now (2024-2025)
3. Key players (both established and emerging)
4. Biggest challenges or pain points in this market
5. Where the opportunities are

Context:
- I'm an electrician considering adding solar installation services
- My focus is on single-family homes
- Geography: Southwestern US (Arizona, Nevada, California)

Output format:
- Bullet points, not paragraphs
- Include sources where possible
- Flag anything that's speculation vs. data-backed
```

## When to Use

- Evaluating a new business opportunity
- Preparing for a pitch or proposal
- Understanding a client's industry
- Deciding whether to enter a market
